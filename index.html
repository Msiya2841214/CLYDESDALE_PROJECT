CLYDESDALE SENIOR SEC SCHOOL

It stands for Clydesdale Senior Secondary School — a public secondary school

It’s located in Umzimkulu, KwaZulu‑Natal, South Africa.

It’s classified as a public (no‑fee) school and serves high‑school learners.

It's also known for producing high quality students that go on to study at top Universities in South Africa

The school writes national exams, including matric (final year) exams, and results (like pass rate and number of learners) are published online for recent years.
